    f182 Ruled surface

    Description

    Creates a surface by joining 2 curves with straight lines (rulers).

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    sur_ruled(id, ref1, ref2);

    Comments

    The parametrisation of the curves is used as the U-parameter of the surface and V goes from the first curve to the second.
