    f14 Create 3D coordinate system


    3D coordinate system


    Description

    Creates a coordinate system with name, position and rotation around the X-, Y- and Z-axis.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    csys_1p(id, name, pos, rot_x, rot_y, rot_z);

    Comments

    The orientation of the coordinate system is computed by first translating it to pos and then rotating it around its own X, Y and Z. X first and then Y and finally Z.

    See also f106 , f107 and f177 for activation and deactivation of coordinate systems.
