    f075 Create cur_comp

    Description

    Creates a curve by copying one or many lines, arcs or curves into a new "composite" curve. cur_comp requires that the entities used to create the new curve are connected in the order they are specified. The first entity must be connected in either end to the second entity and the second entity must be connected to either end of the third entity and so on. Entities may be connected start-to-end or end-to-end or end-to-start or start-to-start (any of the 4 possible ways). Varkon automatically reverses all entities with the wrong sense of direction so that the parametrisation of the resulting new curve becomes continous. Continuity in slope (tangent direction) is not required.

    Available in

    2D and 3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    cur_comp();

    Comments

    None.
