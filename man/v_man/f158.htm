    f158 Rotational surface

    Description

    Creates a surface by rotating a curve around an axis. The curve can be any planar curve including UV-curves and offset curves. The rotational axis is specified by two positions in the same plane as the curve. Rotational angles are measured positive in counterclockwise direction if sitting in pos1 looking at pos2.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    sur_rot(id, ref, pos1, pos2, ang1, ang2, dir);

    Comments

    The parametrisation of the rotaded curve is used as the U-parameter of the surface. V goes from ang1 to ang2 or opposite if direction is reversed (dir = -1).
