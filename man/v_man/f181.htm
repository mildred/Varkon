    f181 Swept surface

    Description

    Creates a surface by sweeping one curve along another another curve.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    sur_sweep(id, ref1, ref2, reverse);

    Comments

    The parametrisation of the swept curve is used as the U-parameter of the surface.
