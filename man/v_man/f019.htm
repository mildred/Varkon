    f19 Create 3D point

    Description

    Creates a 3D point entity. Any X-, Y- and Z-coordinate may be specified.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    poi_free(id, pos);

    Comments

    See also f20 and f21 for other ways to create a point.
