    Hatching

    VARKON includes a special entity for hatching called xhatch. In MBS it looks like this....

    xhatch(id,spacing,angle,id_1,id_2,......id_n);

    xhatch creates a pattern of parallel lines with a given angle to the X-axis of the active coordinate system and a given spacing. The pattern is bounded by one or more lines, arcs or curves and may have interior islands or holes.

    Hatchlines can be solid, dashed or centerlines depending on XFONT and of any dashlength depending on XDASHL.
