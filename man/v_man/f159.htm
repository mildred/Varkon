    f159 Offset surface

    Description

    Creates a surface with constant offset to another surface. A positive offset places the new surface on that side of the old surface in which its normal points. A negative offset places the new surface on the opposite side.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    sur_offs(id, ref, distance);

    Comments

    Any type of surface including offset surfaces can be used to create a new offset surface.
