    f6 Trim entity

    Description

    Clips or extends the end of a line or arc to the intersection with a line, arc or curve. First pick the end of the entity to trim and then indicate the limiting entity. If more than one intersection exists VARKON asks for the intersection number. See the intersect routine in MBS for more info on positive and negative intersect numbers.

    Available in

    2D.

    MBS Correspondence

    The trimmed entity gets a BLANK=1 attribute +

    trim(ref1, end, ref2, alt); +

    unblank(ref1);

    Comments

    None.
