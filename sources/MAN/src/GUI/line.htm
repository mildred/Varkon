Lines

A line in Varkon is straight and limited by start- and end position. The parametrisation along a line is 0 to 1 and the parameter is relative to line length.

Methods

  • Two positions free
  • Two positions projected
  • Parallell
  • Position and angle
  • Position and perpendicular
  • Position and tangent
  • Two tangents

There are no infinite or semi infinite line entities in Varkon. If you need something with direction and infinite length you can use a coordinate system or a vector parameter or variable.

A line can be converted to a curve using the composite curve method.
