    f64 Create 2D circular fillet

    Description

    Creates a circular fillet arc with specified radius. Two crossing lines, arcs or curves or any combination of these may be used to define the fillet. Wich one of the four possible fillet "corners" to use is controlled by picking the two entities involved on that side of the entity that faces the right corner.

    Available in

    2D.

    MBS Correspondence

    arc_fil(id, ref1, ref2, radius, alt);

    Comments

    The radius of an arc must always be greater than zero.
