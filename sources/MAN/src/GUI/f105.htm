    f104 Create 3D coordinate system

    Description

    Creates a coordinate system with name and three positions. The first position is the origin. Second position controls the direction of the X-axis and third position controls the orientation of the XY-plane. The resulting coordinate system will be placed in pos1 with its positive X-axis running through pos2 and with pos3 contained in its XY-plane.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    csys_3p(id, name, pos1, pos2, pos3);

    Comments

    See f106 and f107 for activation and deactivation of coordinate systems.
