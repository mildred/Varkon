    Creating surfaces

    MBS includes a number of methods to create surfaces of different types. The following are available also through the menus of the interactve system:

    sur_rot - Creates a surface by rotating a curve around an axis.

    sur_cyl - Creates a surface by sweeping a curve along a straight line.

    sur_sweep - Creates a surface by sweeping a curve along another curve.

    sur_ruled - Creates a surface by joining two curves with a straight line.

    sur_offs - Creates a surface at constant offset from another surface.
