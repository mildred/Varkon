    f160 Cylindrical surface

    Description

    Creates a surface by linear translation of a curve. The curve can be any planar curve including UV-curves and offset curves. Translation is specified by a positive or negative distance perpendicular to the plane of the curve.

    Available in

    3D.

    MBS Correspondence

    sur_cyl(id, ref, dist, reverse);

    Comments

    The parametrisation of the translated curve is used as the U-parameter of the surface. V goes from the plane of the original curve to the plane at the specified distance or opposite if reverse = 1.
