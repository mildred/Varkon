    f63 Create 2D circular arc with offset

    Description

    Creates a circular arc with same origin and angles as another arc but with different radius. A positive offset gives an arc with larger radius and a negative offset creates a smaller arc.

    Available in

    2D.

    MBS Correspondence

    arc_offs(id, ref, offset);

    Comments

    The radius of an arc must always be greater than zero.
